ESL Roadtrip in France

Since the launch of its new visual identity in late 2014, ESL has been trying to find new ways to reach its audience and refresh its image. Innovating in our communication and marketing: that's exactly what our April 2015 promotional road trip in France was about.

ESL has always endeavoured to be as close to its potential clients as possible by meeting them where they are: in schools, in universities, at specialised fairs, in their workplaces, in their cities. Depending on the target audience, the role of the marketing team is to find ways to bring the right message to the right people in the right places.
